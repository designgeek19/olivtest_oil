About Olivtest Planet

Olivtest Planet Energy Limited is your complete energy alternative and partner for reliable diesel and engine oil supply across Nigeria. We serve businesses and individuals with petroleum products in large, medium, and small volumes.

We also offer on-site services including tank cleaning, diesel generator servicing, parts replacement, and accurate calibration. Our commitment to integrity, efficiency, and customer satisfaction sets us apart.

Additionally, we operate in the real estate sector, helping clients acquire and develop properties of all sizes across Abuja, Nasarawa, and Benue.
