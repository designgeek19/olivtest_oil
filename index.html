About Olivtest Planet

Olivtest Planet Companies Group is a diversified business conglomerate with a shared vision of excellence, innovation, and sustainable growth. Founded on principles of integrity and customer-centric values, we operate across multiple sectors to deliver comprehensive solutions to businesses and individuals throughout Nigeria.

Our group brings together specialized expertise in energy, real estate, technology, and automotive services, creating synergies that benefit our clients and stakeholders. With a commitment to quality and ethical practices, we're building a legacy of trust and innovation.
